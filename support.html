Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get more free games?
You can watch a rewarded ad to earn 1 free game, or subscribe for unlimited plays.
How do I cancel my subscription?
On Android, go to Google Play → Subscriptions → Imposter Malayalam → Cancel. On iOS, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Imposter Malayalam → Cancel.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings (the gear icon on the home screen) → scroll down → tap Delete Account. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.
I subscribed but still see the free plays counter.
Try signing out and signing back in. If the issue persists, contact us at the email below.
The app is crashing or not working.
Try closing and reopening the app. If it continues, uninstall and reinstall. Contact us if the problem persists.
I was charged but didn't receive the subscription.
Contact us at abinm8@gmail.com with your order ID and we'll resolve it within 48 hours.
