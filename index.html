How to Play

1

Enter player names

Add everyone's names. Best with 3 or more players.

2

Pass & peek

Each player secretly sees their word. The Imposter sees nothing!

3

Give clues

Everyone gives one clue about their word. The Imposter must bluff!

4

Vote & reveal

Vote on who you think the Imposter is. Then reveal the truth!
