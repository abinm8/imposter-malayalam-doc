Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Imposter Game - Malayalam Edition ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Your name, email address, and profile photo (via Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple)

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • PostHog — anonymised usage analytics

We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the app.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using the Delete Account feature in the app.
